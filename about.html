About us

Who are we?

We are GAME TIME | GAME ON who appreciate the real value of a hard working player. We are here not just to help also to inspire other people to play basketball even though some of them has a lack of height and confidence. We treat our Player like a Family that will support them until the end.

Our Goal

Our goal is to help our Players to reach their dream. Even though some of them are not afford to pay our trainings but that's not the problem because we have a free training once the player passed the training examination. We are here to help them to play in a professional league.
